My Journey to Design

Where it all began

My journey into web design began over 20 years ago. At the time, my husband had just graduated from dental school and was preparing to start his own practice. With a limited budget and big dreams, I took it upon myself to build our first website using an online web builder.

While it was a great starting point, I quickly realized the platform’s limitations—I wanted something more personal, more unique. That desire led me to learn HTML and CSS so I could create a website that truly reflected our brand and values.

As I dove deeper, my passion for web design naturally expanded into graphic design. I began creating logos, business cards, flyers, postcards—you name it, I designed it! Everything I created was aimed at helping our business stand out and succeed.

Now, with years of hands-on experience and a deep love for design, I’m excited to help you bring your vision to life with something beautiful, distinctive, and uniquely yours.
